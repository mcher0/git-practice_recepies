Chicken pasta

Description

This chicken pasta skillet meal is a tasteful combination of vegetables, chicken, and pasta prepared in a cast iron skillet. My family eats tons of this stuff.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts (cubed)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 12 oz penne pasta
  • 1 tsp each: paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella, ½ cup grated Parmesan

Ingredients

  1. Cook Chicken: Heat oil in a large skillet, season chicken with paprika, salt, and pepper, and cook until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. Make Sauce: In the same skillet, sauté garlic, add tomatoes, broth, cream, and Italian seasoning. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add Pasta: Stir in penne, cover, and simmer until pasta is tender (10-12 min).
  4. Combine: Add chicken back to the skillet, stir in mozzarella and Parmesan. Cook until cheese melts.
